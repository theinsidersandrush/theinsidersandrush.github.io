The Insider Sandrush's 6th Year Anniversary (aka The Insider Sandrush @VI)

6 years, and rolling.

It's been 6 years since I started my channel on pure cringe, and nonsense bullshit. For the past 6 years, I've changed, absolutely a lot. I learned more about the Internet, and how to actually getting around it.

  1. 8 February 2020: The Insider Sandrush was launched as The Kim Possible Channel.
  2. 3 November 2020: Livestreamed for the first time.
  3. 14 November 2020: Created a new channel: The Insider Sandrush Extra
  4. 8 February 2021: Celebrated the first year anniversary.
  5. 22 February 2021: Acquired a Dell Precision M4500. With this, more advanced content is now available.
  6. 21 March 2021: Published a full replay of interview of Janna Nick and Dini Schatzmann for their new drama Marry Me Senorita on "Bawang Live", a Malay language talk show from Astro.
  7. 27 April 2021: The Kim Possible Channel rebranded as The Insider Sandrush, across all social media platforms.
  8. 18 August 2021: The Insider Sandrush receives a new logo branding.
  9. 8 February 2022: Celebrated the second year anniversary.
  10. 2 March 2022: Uploaded the first video on The Insider Sandrush Extra channel.
  11. 20 November 2022: Activities are suspended for the time being due to power cord failure, especially on a Dell Precision M4500.
  12. 8 February 2023: Celebrated the third year anniversary.
  13. 26 February 2023: Activities are resumed after months of inactivity.
  14. 4 October 2023: theinsidersandrush.github.io was launched.
  15. 20 November 2023: Dell Precision M4500 was decommissioned due to permanent damage on power and the CPU was basically fried.
  16. 8 February 2024: The Insider Sandrush announces his YouTube hiatus on YouTube.
  17. 16 March 2024: An NEC laptop and a Toshiba laptop is now a permanent replacement for the broken Dell Precision M4500.
  18. 5 February 2025: After 363 days on inactivity, The Insider Sandrush makes a comeback with 5 videos.
  19. 16 August 2025: After 635 days of decommission, I've finally acquired a Dell Latitude 3400. This time, with a proper GPU.
  20. 9 November 2025: Attempted the livestream for final volume of Blue Archive.
  21. 8 February 2026: Celebrated the sixth year anniversary, known as "The Insider Sandrush @VI"
