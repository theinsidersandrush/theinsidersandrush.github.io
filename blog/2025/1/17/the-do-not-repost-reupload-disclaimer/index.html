The reason(s) why.

There could be reasons why most small (and some known) artists, primarily on the Japanese cluster, start saying that:

The problem that comes with it

As you might have noticed, many artists starting to delete their old posts. Added with the "Do Not Repost / Reupload" signal, and some take it to an another level.

Take note that the artist mentioned (minusT) doesn't have any art deletion requests at this time of writing.
