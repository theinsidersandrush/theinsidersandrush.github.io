The dilemma of choosing between Kivotos and Gensokyo: Blue Archive, Touhou, or both?

Published on Sunday, 31 August 2025, during 68th National Day (Malaysia), Hatsune Miku's birthday, and Suzumi Morizuki's birthday. (UTC +08:00)

A conflict of interests between these two.

Hi. Recently, I've got into Blue Archive completely after owning a Dell Latitude 3400. If you've noticed, since April 2025, I've been extremely leaning towards Blue Archive, which at that time, during a year without a game release from ZUN himself, until he announced "Touhou 20: Fossilized Wonders." in May.

What happened?

During January 2025, I've started to liking Blue Archive, even more, starting with Shiroko and Mari. Since then, my interests on Touhou is extremely plumetting.

Factors on the side of Touhou community for me

  1. Social media: Due to hardships on Bluesky, and lack of discovery of ANY content. I'm not going extremely political, because I hate it so much, it messes with my brain. Also, across the Internet, I felt that yuri literally outpaces hetero/straight art on both Pixiv and Danbooru. There are still some artists who are willing to still draw Touhou characters fall in love with a guy, but many of the decided to quit drawing Touhou alltogether, and move to other fandom.

  2. It's a little bit of controversy: Sometime people tend to do something nonsense. On April last year (2024), Perldrop uploaded a YouTube video, then it got removed by the footage owner on YouTube via means of worldwide copyright claim, then expresses the dissatisfaction on Twitter. Since then, my mental completely broke.

  3. My views on everything: Due to my presumably degrading mental health, I'm currently extremely conflicted on practically everything.

Now, where are you now?

For now, I'd stay on...

Both.
