Recently, if you scrolled Google search long enough to find some memes and a rabbit hole (do you think there will be some Hatsune Miku sex? NO. Not now. Let me listen to some high quality music, please.), you might get some results from Twitter.

The problem

As you might have noticed, even old Tweets that are Tweeted before 25 July 2023 (or 17 May 2024), it's still going to be indexed with "X.com" links.

Here's some example with Jack Dorsey's first Tweet on Twitter on Google Search results.



The solution

For old tweets, just don't include the X.com link. Keep it as it is, PLEASE.

That's all.
