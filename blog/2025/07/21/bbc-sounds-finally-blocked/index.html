Breaking up my major relationship with the BBC: The BBC Sounds, is no more (at least not for most domestic radios).

Published on Monday, 21 July 2025.

Hello, I'm The Insider Sandrush. I'm here sadly to announce to everyone, that: The BBC Sounds, is no longer with us, the outsiders.

What happened?

In case you didn't know what's happening, you can read it here.

What's now?

Starting today (Monday, 21st July), attempts to visit the BBC Sounds website, will be redirected to BBC Audio.

Here's what you're going to see when visiting BBC Sounds' domestic radio pages:

1. Realised there's no "Schedule" button, and the recommended podcasts, right?

2. The website redirects by itself.

3. When you're trying to listening to shows with copyrighted material, it force redirects you there, and telling you a 404.

Conclusion

I've finally had enough. No more BBC for the time being (except for World Service and the dance crazes on Radio 1 Dance). Now, ABC Australia is the only option I have that are not a pain.

Thank you for reading.

~ Sand, 21 July 2025.
