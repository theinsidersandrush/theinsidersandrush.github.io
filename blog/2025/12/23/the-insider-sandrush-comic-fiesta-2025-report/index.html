[Report] Comic Fiesta 2025: First time visiting Art Market, not thinking about finding Touhou.

Published on Tuesday, 23 December 2025.

The boring might be hitting back like a brick, but at least it's a really good time.

Hi, it's The Insider Sandrush.

On Sunday, 21st December 2025, I attended this year's Comic Fiesta again accompanied by my sister, who were probably hanging around Suria KLCC.

Since this is my second time going to a convention surrounded by lots of cosplayers and artists, here's how it goes:

Heading towards KLCC

For this time, I need to eat some food, so I had to go with my family to a kenduri. I only eat Nasi minyak, with fried chicken, and to finish it up... some junk food!!.

I arrived, at KLCC.

After few moments later, I moved and actually already arrived at KLCC's entrance. Here's how it looks.

When I walk up, for a little bit, I eventually able to find my way out to the walking areas. Then, I saw someone talking about a recent Tweet made by PETA that looks like this (CONTENT WARNING). I ignored that, then I just walk in, heading towards the entrance way.

Weirdly this year, there isn't any rainings happening, despite it's the season.

Since the Malaysian Touhou Brigade just moved their gathering day to Saturday, I quickly forgot my plan to look at Symphony Lake, and I just straight to lining up, entering the convention center.

Here's the pic:


Lining up is not that tired btw...

I'm already in...

After unusual and a little bit short lines and relatively almost empty scanning ticket/pass, I finally entered, once again.

For sometimes, I actually going to look at some booths to see what they've got to offer. There's one from Acer with sponsor from Intel bruh, at the same place. Here's a video clip about that.

Then, I buy some book at Gempak Starz booth (I picked The second compilation for The Golden Age of Adventures). Just like last year, Kaoru was there as well. However, I've decided to not taking a picture with her because I've never took a picture with an illustrator.

And then, before I exited the building, I decided to check out Art Market because I've never made it to there due to lacking knowledge about directories.

Entering the Art Market

For the first time ever, I've finally entered the Art Market, where there's actually fanarts, doujins, and more, sold there.

So, here's things that I buy there.

  • Center: Art of Suzumi flashbang by Sleepyowl (Jobson). This is the no-text version.

  • Left: Justice Task Force (featuring Koharu) from Blue Archive. This one, I forgot where did I buy that. Sorry!

  • Right: Marisa Kirisame by Matcha_.

  • Other items:
    • Three Gehenna ID cards: Sena, Kirara, and Satsuki.
    • Button of Mari Iochi from Blue Archive, from collection by Artis Berbudaya.

At 6:00pm, I decided to finally getting out of the Art Market after lots of shopping spree for the first time ever. At that time, I need to go find my sister because she was there too, and she found me. But because I found a lot of cosplayers going around (lots of them primarily are from the Genshin Impact community) across the Christmas tree and inside the Suria KLCC, so I ask for my sister to wait for me.

So, I took some pictures with them.

Due to privacy reasons, I can't show these images.

Note: I actually took a picture together with another Furina cosplayer.

Eventually, I settled together with my sister and go back to my home via means of public transport with stuff that I bringing back home.

Boring hits harder than brick, but it's still fun.

Immediately when I arrived at home, I felt like normally happy. I didn't felt depressed like last year, and I'm just ready to spend another three weeks of normal stuff.

End of the word

Thanks for reading this article about my experience attending Comic Fiesta 2025, second year of visiting Comic Fiesta. See you in Comic Fiesta 2026 (if there are any, or if I can make it to there). And also, Merry Christmas to everyone who are celebrating it. Also, bye!

~ Sand, 23 December 2025.
