[Experience] Three days after going to Comic Fiesta 2024.

Published on Wednesday, 25 December 2024.

My depression level turned up to OVER 9000!!!! (dead meme) after attending it and now I'm bored.

Hi, It's The Insider Sandrush.

On Sunday, 22nd December 2024, I (myself, with my autistic stuff on me) attended this year's (2024) Comic Fiesta.

Since this is my first time going to a convention surrounded by lots of cosplayers and artists, with my autistic brain and my shyness to girls (yes), here's how it goes:

Heading towards KLCC

For this time, I need to eat some food, so I just go to the restaurant somewhere in Kampung Bharu. At least the food is good though.

I arrived, at KLCC.

After few moments later, I moved and actually already arrived at KLCC's entrance. Here's how it looks.

When I walk up, for a little bit, I eventually able to find my way out to the Symphony Lake to see myself for just a bit.

And then, the rain hits me and it slowly becomes bit stronger, but that reduces for some later.

Since I don't see any Touhou cosplayers out there at the designated place (the Symphony Lake), I just join the line up entering the convention center.

Here's the pic:


Lining up is sooooo tired...

I'm already in...

After long lines and scanning ticket/pass, I finally entered for the first time!!

For sometimes, I actually going to look at some booths to see what they've got to offer. There's one from Acer with sponsor from Intel bruh. Here's a video clip about that.

Then, I tried to buy some autograph board at Gempak Starz booth (I picked one from Prince Series). I was thinking about to buy that to get signed by the artist (Kaoru), but it's already closed. How unfortunate me. Don't be like me.

And then, before I exited the building, I buyed something from the organiser's official booth, one being a cosplay book taken during the 2010s or so.

Lastly, I look up the booth if people were selling some Jujutsu Kaisen merchandise, then my friend asked me for a specific one: A Yuta Okkotsu keychain!

I already promised to myself that I'm going to buy some Reimu and Marisa keychain, later to found out that there's none.

Exiting the building, heading to Suria KLCC and then get lost and eventually get back.

After sometimes, I decided to go out to see if I can spot any Touhou cosplayers, but find none. But I found one guy promoting Ceres Fauna and actually kinda feel like: "lol I'll do it"

Then because it's almost 1700 (17:00 / 5:00 p.m.), I need to go back to the place where I started the journey. But because I found a lot of cosplayers going around (lots of them primarily are from the Genshin Impact community) across the Christmas tree and inside the Suria KLCC.

So, I took some pictures with them.

Due to privacy reasons, I can't show these images that easily.

Note: I actually took pictures with three people who were cosplaying as Furina from Genshin Impact. Yeah.

Eventually, I settled alone and go back to my home with that cosplay book and the Prince Series autograph board, not finding anything Touhou...

Depression hits harder than brick

Immediately when I arrived at home, I felt like I'm so happy and also kind of depressed so much. It's concerning that I just want to go back sooooooooooo much.

End of the word

Thanks for reading this article about my experience attending Comic Fiesta 2024. Hope I can go again in 2025... see you next year.

~ Sand, 25 December 2024.
