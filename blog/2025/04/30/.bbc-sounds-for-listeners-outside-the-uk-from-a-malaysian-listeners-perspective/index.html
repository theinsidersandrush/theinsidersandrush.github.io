[The State of Touhou Malaysia] Temporary closure of Touhou Malaysia.

Published on Thursday, 13 March 2025.

We are leaving, until when time is good...

Hello, I'm The Insider Sandrush, the owner of Touhou Malaysia on Twitter, YouTube, and Bluesky.

What's that?

In case you didn't aware about what am I talking about, here's a rundown.

Touhou Malaysia is a gimmick account project that I started in November 2023 on Twitter and on 2 December 2023 on Bluesky, where I post/Tweet Touhou Project related stuff in Malay/Bahasa Melayu.

Like anything else, I also tend to break the character by Tweeting some unnecessary/off-topic stuff.

When Leaving Twitter

Starting 15 March 2025, the Touhou Malaysia project is over and will not be resumed until further notice. Thank you for the stay during November 2023 until 18 May 2024.

Thank you for reading.

~ Sand, 13 March 2025.
